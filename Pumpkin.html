Pumpkin carving ideas

Getting started

Before you start carving your amazing detailed pumpkins its importnat that you know proper saftey rules.

Be sure you never carve witht he knife facing you, have some kind of sheet under the pumpkin so the pumpkin goo doesn't get all over the place.

Dont carve the whole thing

Instead of cutting the whole thing just cut some around it, making it seem like the pumpkin has teeth!

 


Don't use JUST a pumpkin

Instead of the usuall round pumpkins, use a squash! there are all kinds of pumpkin like things you can use to make cool carvings.

Make it a picture

This might be hard, but try carving a painting on your pumpkin instead of a face, Its more eye-catching if it's good and you'll feel really proud once it's done.
